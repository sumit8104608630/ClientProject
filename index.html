Project Highlights

RealEstate Prime is a premium residential project located in the heart of the city, offering luxurious 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments with world-class amenities. Designed by award-winning architects, our project focuses on modern living with sustainability at its core.

Each apartment features spacious rooms, high-quality finishes, and large windows that provide ample natural light and ventilation. Our homes are designed to provide a perfect blend of comfort, style, and functionality.

With 24/7 security, power backup, and modern infrastructure, RealEstate Prime offers a secure and comfortable living environment for you and your family. The project is surrounded by lush green spaces, creating a serene atmosphere away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Prime Location & Connectivity

RealEstate Prime is strategically located with excellent connectivity to all major parts of the city. The project is just 15 minutes away from the central business district and 20 minutes from the international airport.

Several renowned educational institutions, including international schools and colleges, are within a 5 km radius. Healthcare facilities with multi-specialty hospitals are just a 10-minute drive away.

The upcoming metro station is only 1 km from the project, providing seamless connectivity across the city. Major shopping malls, entertainment centers, and fine dining restaurants are easily accessible from the location.
