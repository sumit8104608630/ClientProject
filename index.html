Project Highlights

ISTAY BY STARWING (ANDHERI (E), MAROL) is a premium residential address offering thoughtfully planned 1 & 2 BHK balcony homes inside a secure 21-storey gated community. With the Marol Metro just 2 mins away, 50+ lifestyle amenities, and Possession in Dec 2027, it blends everyday convenience with calm, behind Seven Hills Hospital.

Each home is designed for zero-wastage space with abundant natural light and ventilation, Vaastu-compliant (East–West) planning, and private balconies. Smart kitchens, efficient storage, and practical room sizes make life easy. Sample flats are ready, and Jodi options are available for growing families.

With 24×7 security, access-controlled entry, power backup, and modern infrastructure, the community delivers a safe, comfortable environment surrounded by landscaped greens, sky lounges, and family play pockets—close to Andheri's work hubs yet tucked away from the noise. Choose ISTAY BY STARWING (ANDHERI (E), MAROL) for a home that balances Luxury • Serenity • Affordability.

Prime Location & Connectivity OF ISTAY BY STARWING (ANDHERI (E), MAROL)
